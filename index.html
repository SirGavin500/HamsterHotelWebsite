Welcome to a Hamster Paradise

Nestled in the heart of Cavia California, La Petite Maison is a luxury hotel designed for the small mammal with extravagance in mind. Escape to the warm comforts of stunning rooms, a relaxing spa, and a pool with a beach side view.

Inspired by the warm beauty of the California Coast, we invite you to enjoy the rich accommodations along with our unmatched luxury. Discover the satisfaction of indulging in our quality rooms, and thank you for choosing La Petite Maison.

Packages

Beach Wedding Package

Starting US $1,500.00

Feel the warmth of the beach as the flames in your hearts ignite during our romantic beach side wedding. All wedding packages include catering, an open bar, and one tier wedding cake. A private dinner for your closest family members and friends will be provided after the ceremony.

Full Bloom Package

Starting US $1,000.00

Enjoy the shade of our cherry trees as you join us during our spring celebration. We invite you to our orchards for fruit picking that is open all day. We end each evening with fresh, vegetable-centric dinners and live music.

Cheese and Seed Tour Package

Starting $1,200.00

Immerse yourself in the flavors of Cavia with our Cheese and Seed tour. Enjoy world-class cheeses from our local creamery. Cheese and seeds are served all day, followed by nightly spa treatments.
